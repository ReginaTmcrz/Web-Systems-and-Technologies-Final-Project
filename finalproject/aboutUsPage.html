Meet the amazing and dedicated people behind Chooks-To-Go. Their hard work and perseverance in constantly improving our products and services is always an inspiration to others in the company.

Mr. Mascariñas is the President and General Manager of Bounty Agro Ventures, Inc. since 2002. He earned his Bachelors degree in Agriculture from the University of the Philippines at Los Baños as a Cocofed scholar and Master in Management degree from the Asian Institute of Management as a full-time scholar of Pure Foods Corporation. Also, he completed a course from the International Law Institute, Washington D.C. in the United States on the Rights & Obligations of Developing Countries under the World Trade Organization. In 2004, he was conferred by his alma mater as Most Outstanding Alumnus in the field of Entrepreneurship. He was also awarded by his peers in the poultry industry a Plaque of Excellence for his outstanding Leadership as the President of the Philippine Association of Broiler Integrators from 1999 to 2001. In 2014, he was awarded as Most Outstanding Professional for 2014 by Superbrands Marketing International, Inc., and in 2015 he garnered the Communications and Leadership award from Toastmasters International, which is the highest award that Toastmasters can bestow to a non-member. Prior to joining BAVI, he served Pure Foods Corporation for 19 years where he started his career and rose through the ranks to become one of the youngest Senior Vice Presidents of Pure Foods Corporation at age 38. He capped his career with Pure Foods Corporation as head of three business units; Poultry, Food Service, and President of the Smokey’s Fast Food Chain. He is an active member of the Makati Business Club and the Management Association of the Philippines. He is a frequent speaker and lecturer in various industry associations and universities.
